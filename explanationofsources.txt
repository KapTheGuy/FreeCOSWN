common.c and common.h include the typical stdio and stdint features. It should be included in every single program, driver, and source file.

descriptor_tables.c and descriptor_tables.h include the GDT and the IDT. Feel free to use them in your project with no credit. They are part
of free information.

monitor.c and monitor.h include typical printing functions.

timer.c and timer.h include a timer driver using the x86 Programmable Interrupt Controller also known as the PIC. Support for APIC, LAPIC, and such
are not included in FreeCOSWN yet. Although they might be implemented very soon.

multiboot.h includes typical GRUB 2 Multiboot Header structures. To easily get a framebuffer and grub modules working.

Feel free to ask any questions using the github issue function.
